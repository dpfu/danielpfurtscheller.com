abstract: This paper deals with fan communication in relation to news media organizations
  and media practitioners. Using a corpus of live tweets posted in parallel to news
  broadcasts by the Austrian Broadcasting Corporation, I examine news-related fan
  practices in the Austrian media landscape. The qualitative analysis reveals how
  “being a news fan” is expressed in social media, elaborates exemplary fan practices,
  and sheds light on the specifics of news-related fan cultures in the interplay of
  legacy and digital media.
